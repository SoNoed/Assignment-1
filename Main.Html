Shark Habitat


The Whale Shark is a slow-moving carpet shark and is the largest known extant fish species.
They are currently classified as Endangered on the Conservation status.
The whale shark inhabits all tropical and warm-temperate seas.
It is primarily pelagic, and can be found in both coastal and oceanic habitats.
